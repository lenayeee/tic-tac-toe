How to Play:

  • Use Arrow Keys to move Pacman
  • Eat dots to score points (10 points each)
  • Eat power pellets to turn ghosts blue (50 points)
  • Eat blue ghosts for bonus points (200 points)
  • Avoid ghosts when they're not blue
  • Clear the maze to advance to next level
  • Game ends when you run out of lives
